Courses

Discover the hidden world that exists just beyond the edge of sight. My macro photography courses are designed for anyone who wants to capture the delicate details of flowers, insects, and the tiny textures of nature. Whether you’re a complete beginner or looking to refine your close‑up skills, these courses will guide you through the techniques that bring small subjects to life. You’ll learn how to control depth of field, master lighting at close range, choose the right lenses, and create beautifully soft bokeh that makes your subject stand out. Each session includes hands‑on practice, clear demonstrations, and personalised guidance to help you grow your confidence behind the camera. If you’ve ever wanted to explore the miniature world in stunning detail, these courses will give you the tools and inspiration to do it.
